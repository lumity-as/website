About Lumity

Lumity AS is an independent consultancy company bringing light to today's darkness of IT complexity. We believe that simple solutions are the key to robust software.

Our name reflects our mission: to bring clarity and understanding to complex technical challenges, just as a lighthouse guides sailors safely through the dark.
